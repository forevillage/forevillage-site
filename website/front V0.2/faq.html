Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to common questions about our websites and how the process works.

Why do I need a website?

A website gives your business a professional home online that you fully control. It helps customers find you, learn about your services, and trust your business before they contact you.

What do I get when I order a website?

You get a clean, professional website customized with your business name, content, and contact information — ready to go live.

Do I need my own domain?

No. Your website can go live immediately on a yourbusiness.forevillage.com subdomain. Of course, you can move to your own domain anytime.

How long does it take?

Most websites are ready within a few days after we receive your content and details.

Can I make changes later?

Yes. We can help update your website, or provide the files if you prefer to manage it yourself.

Is this a monthly subscription?

No. Pricing is simple and transparent. No hidden monthly fees.

What kind of businesses is this for?

Our websites are designed for small businesses such as restaurants, cafés, salons, studios, consultants, and local service providers.
